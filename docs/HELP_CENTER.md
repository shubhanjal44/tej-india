## Getting Started

### What is SkillSwap India?

SkillSwap India is a platform that connects people who want to learn new skills with those willing to teach. Instead of paying money, you exchange skills - you teach something you're good at, and in return, you learn something new!

### How does skill swapping work?

1. **Create your profile** - Add skills you want to teach and skills you want to learn
2. **Find matches** - Browse or search for users who have skills you want and want to learn skills you have
3. **Request a swap** - Send a swap request to someone whose skills interest you
4. **Connect & learn** - Once accepted, schedule sessions and start learning!
5. **Leave reviews** - After completing swaps, rate your experience to help the community

### Is SkillSwap India free?

Yes! The basic platform is completely free. We also offer Premium and Pro subscriptions with additional features like unlimited swaps, priority matching, and advanced analytics.

### What kind of skills can I teach or learn?

Any skill! Popular categories include:
- **Arts & Creativity**: Photography, Painting, Music, Dance
- **Technology**: Programming, Web Design, Digital Marketing
- **Languages**: English, Hindi, Spanish, French
- **Cooking & Culinary**: Indian Cuisine, Baking, Healthy Cooking
- **Fitness & Wellness**: Yoga, Meditation, Personal Training
- **Professional Skills**: Public Speaking, Writing, Business Skills
- **Crafts & Hobbies**: Knitting, Gardening, DIY Projects

### How do I sign up?

1. Visit [skillswap.in](https://skillswap.in)
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email, name, and password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile
6. Add your skills
7. Start finding matches!

## Account Management

### How do I verify my email?

1. Check your inbox for a verification email from SkillSwap India
2. Click the verification link
3. Your account will be verified automatically

**Didn't receive the email?**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Click "Resend verification email" in your account settings
- Contact support if you still don't receive it

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password

### How do I update my profile?

1. Log in to your account
2. Click on your profile picture (top right)
3. Select "Edit Profile"
4. Update your information
5. Click "Save Changes"

### Can I change my email address?

Yes! Go to Settings > Account > Email Address and follow the verification process for your new email.

### How do I delete my account?

**Warning**: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm your password
5. Click "Delete My Account"

All your data, swaps, and messages will be permanently deleted.

## Skills & Learning

### How do I add skills to my profile?

**Adding Skills to Teach:**
1. Go to your profile
2. Click "Add Skill to Teach"
3. Search for the skill or create a new one
4. Select your proficiency level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, Expert)
5. Add years of experience (optional)
6. Write a brief description
7. Click "Save"

**Adding Skills to Learn:**
1. Go to your profile
2. Click "Add Skill to Learn"
3. Search for the skill
4. Select your current level
5. Write what you want to achieve
6. Click "Save"

### What's the maximum number of skills I can add?

- **Free users**: Up to 10 skills (5 to teach, 5 to learn)
- **Premium users**: Up to 20 skills
- **Pro users**: Unlimited skills

### Can I remove skills from my profile?

Yes! Go to your profile, find the skill you want to remove, click the "..." menu, and select "Remove Skill."

### How do proficiency levels work?

- **Beginner**: Just starting, basic understanding
- **Intermediate**: Comfortable with fundamentals, some experience
- **Advanced**: Highly skilled, significant experience
- **Expert**: Professional level, can teach others effectively

### Can I create a new skill if it's not listed?

Yes! When adding a skill, if you can't find it in the search results, click "Create New Skill" and it will be added to the platform.

## Skill Swaps

### How do I find people to swap with?

Several ways to find matches:

1. **Discover Page**: Browse recommended matches based on your interests
2. **Search**: Use filters for skills, location, rating, and availability
3. **Recommendations**: Check your AI-powered skill recommendations
4. **Events**: Attend events to meet people with similar interests
5. **Communities**: Join communities related to your skills

### How do I request a swap?

1. Find a user whose skills interest you
2. Click on their profile
3. Click "Request Swap"
4. Select the skill you want to learn from them
5. Select the skill you want to teach them
6. Write a personalized message
7. Propose a schedule (optional)
8. Click "Send Request"

### What happens after I send a swap request?

The other user will receive a notification. They can:
- **Accept**: Your swap will begin!
- **Decline**: You'll be notified and can find other matches
- **Ignore**: Request expires after 7 days

### How do I accept or decline swap requests?

1. Go to "My Swaps" > "Pending Requests"
2. Click on a request to view details
3. Review the person's profile and skills
4. Click "Accept" or "Decline"
5. If accepting, you'll be able to message them to schedule sessions

### What if a swap request is accepted?

Congratulations! Now you can:
1. Message your swap partner
2. Exchange contact details
3. Schedule your first session
4. Agree on learning goals and structure
5. Start your skill exchange journey!

### How many swaps can I have at once?

- **Free users**: 3 active swaps
- **Premium users**: 10 active swaps
- **Pro users**: Unlimited active swaps

### How do I schedule sessions?

Use the built-in messaging system to:
1. Propose dates and times
2. Agree on session duration (typically 1-2 hours)
3. Choose a location (in-person) or platform (online)
4. Set learning objectives for each session

### How do I mark a session as complete?

1. Go to "My Swaps"
2. Click on the active swap
3. Find the session
4. Click "Mark Complete"
5. Both partners need to confirm completion

### Can I cancel a swap?

Yes, but try to communicate with your partner first.

1. Go to "My Swaps"
2. Click on the swap
3. Click "Cancel Swap"
4. Select a reason
5. Confirm cancellation

**Note**: Frequent cancellations may affect your rating.

### What if I'm not satisfied with a swap?

If you're having issues:
1. **Communicate**: Talk to your swap partner first
2. **Adjust**: Try modifying the schedule or approach
3. **Cancel**: If it's not working out, cancel respectfully
4. **Report**: If there's inappropriate behavior, report the user

## Safety & Trust

### How do you ensure user safety?

We take safety seriously:

1. **Email Verification**: All users must verify their email
2. **Profile Moderation**: New profiles are reviewed
3. **Rating System**: Users rate each other after swaps
4. **Reporting**: Easy-to-use reporting tools
5. **Content Moderation**: AI and human moderation
6. **Privacy Controls**: Control who sees your information

### What should I do if I feel unsafe?

**Immediate danger**: Contact local authorities (100 in India)

**Platform safety concerns**:
1. Stop communication with the person
2. Report the user or content
3. Block the user
4. Contact our safety team: safety@skillswap.in

### How do I report a user?

1. Go to their profile
2. Click "..." menu
3. Select "Report User"
4. Choose a reason:
   - Inappropriate behavior
   - Scam or fraud
   - Harassment
   - Fake profile
   - Other
5. Provide details
6. Submit report

### How do I block someone?

1. Go to their profile
2. Click "..." menu
3. Select "Block User"
4. Confirm

Blocked users cannot:
- View your profile
- Send you messages
- Send you swap requests

### How can I protect my privacy?

**Profile Settings**:
- Control who can see your email and phone number
- Limit who can send you swap requests
- Hide your last active status

**Meeting Safety**:
- First meetings in public places
- Tell a friend where you're going
- Don't share personal financial information
- Trust your instincts

### Are my messages private?

Yes! Messages are encrypted and only visible to you and the recipient. Our moderators only review reported messages.

### How does the verification badge work?

Premium and Pro users can get verified by:
1. Submitting a government ID
2. Verifying phone number
3. Completing at least 5 swaps with 4+ rating

Verified badges help build trust in the community.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in

**Possible solutions**:
1. Check your email and password are correct
2. Try the "Forgot Password" option
3. Clear browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser
5. Check if your account is suspended (check email)
6. Contact support if issue persists

### I'm not receiving emails

**Check**:
1. Spam/junk folder
2. Email address in account settings is correct
3. Add noreply@skillswap.in to contacts
4. Check email notification preferences
5. Try requesting a new verification email

### The app/website is slow

**Try**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try a different browser
5. Update your app (mobile)
6. Check our status page: status.skillswap.in

### I can't upload images

**Requirements**:
- Profile photos: Max 5MB, JPG/PNG/WEBP
- Message attachments: Max 10MB
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP, PDF

**Solutions**:
1. Compress large images
2. Check file format
3. Try a different browser
4. Clear cache
5. Contact support

### My profile isn't showing in search

**Possible reasons**:
1. Profile is incomplete (add skills, bio, photo)
2. Email not verified
3. Account is new (wait 24 hours)
4. Privacy settings restrict visibility
5. Account is suspended

### I'm not getting swap requests

**Tips to improve**:
1. Complete your profile 100%
2. Add a clear profile photo
3. Write a detailed bio
4. Add more skills to teach
5. Be active (log in regularly)
6. Join communities
7. Attend events
8. Upgrade to Premium for profile boost

### Payment failed

**Try**:
1. Check card details are correct
2. Ensure sufficient balance
3. Contact your bank about online payments
4. Try a different payment method
5. Try again after a few minutes
6. Contact support with error details

## Quick Tips for Success

### For Teachers
1. **Be patient**: Everyone learns at different paces
2. **Set clear expectations**: Discuss goals early
3. **Prepare**: Plan your sessions in advance
4. **Be punctual**: Respect your student's time
5. **Encourage**: Positive feedback motivates learning

### For Learners
1. **Be committed**: Show up prepared and on time
2. **Practice**: Regular practice between sessions
3. **Ask questions**: No question is too small
4. **Give feedback**: Help your teacher improve
5. **Be respectful**: Show appreciation for their time

### For Everyone
1. **Complete your profile**: Add photo, bio, and skills
2. **Be responsive**: Reply to messages promptly
3. **Communicate clearly**: Set expectations early
4. **Leave reviews**: Help build community trust
5. **Stay safe**: Meet in public, trust your instincts
6. **Have fun**: Enjoy the learning journey!

## Glossary

**Skill Swap**: An exchange where two people teach each other different skills

**Active Swap**: A swap that's currently in progress

**Proficiency Level**: Your skill level (Beginner to Expert)

**Session**: A single meeting to learn/teach a skill

**Rating**: Score (1-5 stars) given after completing a swap

**Verification Badge**: Symbol showing a user is verified

**SkillCoins**: Virtual currency earned through platform activities (coming soon)

**XP (Experience Points)**: Points earned for completing swaps and activities
